Balut!

A balut is a fertilized bird egg (usually a duck) which is incubated for a period of 14 to 21 days, depending on the local culture, and then steamed.

Ingredients

  • Balut
  • Courage and Asian sensibilities
  • Soy sauce
  • Chilli

Steps

  1. Inspect the Balut
  2. You can eat the yolk, white, and the partially developed duck embryo inside.
