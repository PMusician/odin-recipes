Delicious Isopod Ramen!

A restaurant in Taipei, Taiwan, is now serving a meal featuring a 14-legged sea animal called a giant isopod. The treat has quickly become popular with the public. People are lining up to enjoy a taste, and take pictures of, the giant isopod. It comes in a noodle soup called ramen

Ingredients

  • Isopod member of the cockroach family
  • Ramen noodles
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Chop and prepare all the vegetables.
  2. Cook proteins as needed (sliced and seasoned pork or chicken, tofu, or shrimp).
  3. Cook Ramen Noodles:
  4. Boil a pot of water.
  5. Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  6. Drain and set aside.
  7. Make Broth:
  8. In a separate pot, heat the broth (chicken, pork, or vegetable).
  9. Add seasonings like soy sauce, miso paste, garlic, and ginger to taste.
  10. Allow the broth to simmer, enhancing the flavors.
  11. Assemble the Ramen:
  12. Place a portion of cooked ramen noodles into each serving bowl.
  13. Ladle the hot broth over the noodles.
  14. Add Toppings:
  15. Arrange cooked protein on top of the noodles.
  16. Add prepared vegetables like sliced green onions, mushrooms, spinach, and bean sprouts.
  17. Garnish:
  18. Garnish with nori sheets, sliced soft-boiled eggs, and additional green onions if desired.
  19. Serve Hot:
  20. Serve immediately while the ramen is hot.
